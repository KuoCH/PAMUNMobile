School Registration

This year, overseas students may register as part of a school delegation. This is meant to facilitate important conference communications with overseas delegates who may attend the same school or be members of the same MUN club. Instead of communicating with each delegate individually, important information will be forwarded to that school delegationâ€™s head delegate. There is no advantage or disadvantage to registering as part of a school delegation, and it will have no impact on your country assignments or committee sessions.

School Delegations are only available for delegates who are studying at a post-secondary institution outside of Taiwan. Any delegates studying in Taiwan, whether full-time or on exchange may not register as part of a school delegation. If you are the only overseas delegate coming from your school, you should register as an individual delegate. Please see the Delegate Registration page for more information. If there are additional questions regarding hotel booking, visa applications, and pre-conference planning, feel free to contact the Delegate Affairs Team at delegate.affairs@panasiamun.com.

Head Delegate

Every school delegation must designate one of their delegates as their head delegate. All conference information will be delivered to the head delegate, and he or she is responsible for communicating this information to the other delegates and coordinating their groupâ€™s trip planning. During the registration process, head delegates are also required to complete an additional form to register their school delegation. Please do not complete this form until all the delegates from your school delegation have individually registered first. Required information on this form includes: total number of delegates and names of all the delegates in the school delegation.
