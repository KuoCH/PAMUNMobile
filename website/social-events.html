Social Events

After a long and productive day of committee sessions, draw each of your four evenings with us to a memorable close with our uniquely themed social event. These socials are an integral part of the Pan Asia MUN experience, providing an opportunity to experience the rich culture of traditional and contemporary Taiwan with your fellow delegates.

First Evening: Dinner Banquet
The Imperial Banquet《宮‧艷》
Like their European counterparts, a traditional Chinese feast is a multi-course extravaganza for the senses. We sincerely invite you to join us as we give you a taste of classical Chinese dining experience set in a Chinese banquet hall. As you walk down the red carpet into the venue, taking in the gorgeous sights, the rich aroma, and the traditional music, prepare yourself for a true feast for the senses that marks the first of many memorable nights to come.
Time:
18:30 - 22:00
Venue:
新店彭園會館
Dress Code:
Yellow or purple formal wear (dresses for women and suits for men). Bring out your best evening wear in yellow and purple, or accessorize with these two Chinese Aristocratic colors!
Address:
新北市新店區北新路三段219號2樓

Second Evening: Featured Party
Absolute Zero 冰鏡華緣
No winter is complete without the frosty nip of snow melting on your cheeks. This December, put on your winter coats and grab your gloves as Pan Asia MUN transports you into a magical world of ice. Don’t be afraid of the cold however, as there are ample activities to with your friends to keep warm. Feel free to build a snowman, enjoy the hot chocolate, or tear up the ice rink on your skates. With snowflakes falling around you, submerge yourself in the thrill of the cold as your experience a winter the likes of which Taipei has never seen.
Time:
18:30 - 22:00
Venue:
西門北極熊滑冰世界
Dress Code:
Fluffy and warm accessories (gloves required for skating). Put on something fluffy and warm. Picture a knitted sweater or ear muffs. Gloves are mandatory for those who wish to skate!
Address:
台北市萬華區漢中街116號8樓
Show it on map
Third Evening: Committee Dinner
A Taste of Taipei 舌尖上的台北
As one of the gourmet capitals of the world, Taipei offers a mouthwatering array of delicious eats. This ranges from your traditional selection of local delicacies to a growing fare of classy and modern fusion dishes. For your third night with us, join fellow delegates on a culinary adventure at one of our partner restaurants. You will be able to experience Eastern cuisine in style during your savory and exciting night on the town.
Time:
17:00 - 20:00
Venue:
TBA by Committee
Dress Code:
None. There is no dress code for the committee dinner! Enjoy a relaxing and sociable dinner with your fellow delegates and dais members at a restaurant we have carefully chosen.
Final Evening: Night Club
Neon Splash 螢色染夜
Join us for a final yet unforgettable night at one of Taipei's premier nightclubs featuring complimentary beverages and a professional DJ. This year’s theme is Neon, so shed the suits, don your fluorescents and paint the night with color and vivid memories. As the spirit flows through your veins and the music charges your soul, let the memories of an unforgettable conference etch into your heart forever as you party the night away.
Time:
21:00 - 1:30
Venue:
HIVE Club
Dress Code:
Neon club wear. Wear a neon outfit or neon accessories that will stand out in the black light.
Address:
台北市信義區松壽路12號 (Att 4 fun)
