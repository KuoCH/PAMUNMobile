Letter

Dear Delegates,

It is our great honor and pleasure to extend the warmest welcome to you to Pan Asia Model United Nations 2014 on behalf of the members of the Department of Academics. In the months leading up to the conference, the members of the Department of Academics have fully devoted themselves to preparations for the conference to provide you an unparalleled Model United Nations experience. It is our sincere hope that you will enjoy the four days of heated debates and caucuses in the lively city of Taipei.

Within the past few decades, Model United Nations has become one of the most invigorating and intensive worldwide activities that facilitates and accommodates students with the essence of tackling international issues and negotiating transnational disputes. This unparalleled simulation allows participants to explore some of the most interesting topics from the past, present, and future. Since its inaugural year, Pan Asia Model United Nations has strived to provide delegates with a variety of simulated committees with a unique Asian touch.

This year’s conference offers seven committees of varying fields of international issues and topics to cater to both first-time and veteran delegates. Apart from committees affiliated with the main organs of the United Nations, regional organizations such as the ASEAN Regional Forum (ARF) and Shanghai Cooperation Organization (上合組織), and the newly introduced crisis committee, United States National Security Council (USNSC), will also be stimulated to provide delegates with the opportunity to expand their fields of exploration within the Model United Nations society.

We would like to again express our utmost and sincere welcome to you for participating Pan Asia Model United Nations 2014. Should there be any questions, doubts, or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us at the email address below. We look forward to meeting you all this December!


Sincerely,

Ederson Chang and Joseph Ban
Under Secretary Generals of Academics
Pan Asia Model United Nations 2014
