Payment Information

Delegate Fees

Early registration fee: NTD $3700
Regular registration fee: NTD $4000

Payment Methods

For both international and Taiwanese delegates:
Credit card
Paypal

For Taiwanese delegates only:
ATM transfer

All fees will be processed through our registration service provider, KKTIX. Delegates must complete payment within 5 days of submitting their registration form. Registration is not considered complete until payment is made, and your unprocessed form will be reset after these five days.

繳費方式有3種: 信用卡 (適用於國內及國外代表)
Paypal (適用於國內及國外代表)
銀行轉帳 (僅適用於國內代表)

請於填寫報名表後五天內完成付款，未付款者將由系統自動取消報名資格。

Hotel Booking Services

After completing the registration process and payment, those who require hotel accommodations will be directed to our Hotel Booking Service page. Delegates may book at a discounted price with one of our partner hotels, and PAMUN will handle the reservation and payment process for you. More information about pricing and hotel options will be released soon. Please check the Hotel Information page to stay up to date.

Refunds

Please see our Conference Policies for more details regarding our refund policy.
