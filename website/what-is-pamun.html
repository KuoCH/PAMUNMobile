What is Pan Asia MUN

What is Pan Asia Model United Nations?

One of the leading conference of its kind in Taiwan, PAMUN continues to devote itself to providing delegates with the best MUN experience possible. Pan Asia MUN 2013 brought together over 370 delegates from more than 20 different nations. This year's conference continues to expand our delegate base in order to provide delegates with a truly diverse cross-cultural experience. Undergraduate and graduate students gather for four days to discuss, debate, and attempt to solve a variety of international issues. Delegates will also explore the unique culture of historical and modern Taiwan through our four social events.

This year's conference will be held from 12/4 - 7, 2014 in Taipei, Taiwan.

History

Past Secretary Generals:

2013 - Frances Lee 李欣樺

Pan Asia Model United Nations is one of the largest collegiate Model United Nations held in East Asia. After hosting WorldMUN 2010 in Taipei with the Harvard Secretariat, members of the original National Taiwan University Host Team sought to create a new annual regional conference that was similar in scope and vigor. From this desire, Pan Asia MUN was formed in the winter of 2010 to embody the spirit of Model United Nations and provide a diverse, engaging experience for local and international students alike.
