Committees and Topics

ASEAN Regional Forum (ARF)
Topic I: The development and expansion of cross-border intellectual workforce in Southeast Asia
Topic II: Impact of immigration, migration, and refugee policies in Southeast Asia

Disarmament and International Security (DISEC)
Topic I: Prevention of an arms race and peaceful uses of outer space
Topic II: Eliminating illicit trade and usage of small arms and light weapons

United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO)
Topic I: Building and strengthening resilience on coastal and small islands
Topic II: Promoting intercultural dialogue for sustainable development

United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime (UNODC)
Topic I: Elimination of violence against women and children
Topic II: Protection of the rights of those facing the death penalty

United Nations Security Council (UNSC)
Topic I: Situation in Northern Mali
Topic II: Response mechanism for possible genocide

United States National Security Council (USNSC)
The United States National Security Council (USNSC) is a continuous crisis committee, making it distinctively different from the other committees at PAMUN 2014. In this committee, delegates’ decisions and actions will immediately affect the perpetual crisis being simulated

SCO 上海合作組織 (中文委員會)
議題壹：深化區域安全合作
議題貳：能源安全合作

Please see our Committees section for more detailed introductions to each committee.
