Welcome from SG

Dear delegates and friends of Pan Asia MUN,

On behalf of the PAMUN organizing team, we warmly invite you to join us from December 04 to December 07, 2014 in Taipei, Taiwan for Pan Asia Model United Nations 2014.

As the leading conference of its kind in Taiwan, PAMUN is devoted to providing delegates with the best MUN experience possible. Pan Asia MUN 2013 brought together over 370 delegates of more than 20 different nationalities. This year’s conference continues to expand our delegate base in order to provide our delegates with a truly diverse cross cultural experience. Undergraduate and graduate students gather for four days to discuss, debate, and attempt to solve a variety of issues, including international security, political, and humanitarian concerns. Delegates also explore the diverse and unique culture of Taiwan through our four social events.

After serving as one of the Under Secretary Generals of Academics for PAMUN 2013, I am humbled and honored to be returning as the Secretary General for this year’s conference. I hope you will join me at Pan Asia Model United Nations 2014, as it is sure to be an unforgettable journey of profound debate, self-discovery, and lasting friendships.

If there are any additional questions, comments, or concerns, please visit our Visit Us or Mail Us page for the relevant contact information, or feel free to contact me personally at joyce.lee@panasiamun.com.

We look forward to seeing you in December in Taipei!

Sincerely,

Joyce Lee
Secretary General
