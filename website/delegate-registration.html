Delegate Registration

Every delegate, whether part of a school delegation or a double delegation, is required to fill out every section on the the PAMUN 2014 Registration Form. There are several sections on the registration form, including school delegation information, personal information, emergency contact, committee preferences, and double delegation information. Delegates will be provided with the payment page after all required sections have been completed and submitted and registration is not considered complete until all required fees have been received. Please see the Payment page for more information regarding fees and payment options.

Double Delegations

A double delegation consists of two delegates who represent the same country in the same committee. All delegates wishing to be part of a double delegation must find their double delegation partner before registering for the conference. Delegates who wish to be part of a double delegation but do not fill out a partner name in the registration form will be considered a single delegation.

School Registration

If you are an overseas student who would like to register as part of a school delegation, please check the School Delegation page for more information. Please note that registration as part of a school delegation is only available to overseas students.

Specialized Committees

In the registration form, delegates will have the chance to fill out your preferences for committees based on interest. Two of our seven committees, SCO上海合作組織 and United States National Security Council (USNSC), are application-based committees, meaning a separate application essay is required. The topics for these essays can be found on the respective committee pages. We would also like to remind you that SCO is a Chinese-Language Committee, meaning committee sessions and official documents will all be in Mandarin-Chinese.
